LumiqTrace offers four plans — Free, Pro, Team, and Scale — each with a fixed monthly event quota, data retention window, and limits on projects and members. Billing is managed through Stripe; you can upgrade, downgrade, update your payment method, and download invoices all from within the LumiqTrace dashboard.

## Plans at a glance

| | Free | Pro | Team | Scale |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Price** | $0/mo | $29/mo | $99/mo | $299/mo |
| **Events/month** | 10K | 100K | 1M | 10M |
| **Data retention** | 7 days | 30 days | 90 days | 1 year |
| **Projects** | 1 | 3 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Members** | 1 | 1 | Up to 10 | Unlimited |
| **Overage** | Events dropped | $0.50 / 10K | $0.50 / 10K | $0.30 / 10K |

## Overage billing

On the **Pro** and **Team** plans, if your organization exceeds the monthly event quota, additional events are billed at **$0.50 per 10,000 events**. On the **Scale** plan, the overage rate is **$0.30 per 10,000 events**.

On the **Free** plan, there is no overage — once you hit 10,000 events in a billing month, additional events are dropped and not recorded. Upgrade to Pro or higher if you need to capture all events.

<Note>
  Overage charges are calculated at the end of each billing period and added to your next invoice. You can monitor your current event count against your quota in **Settings > Billing** at any time.
</Note>

## Viewing current usage

Go to **Settings > Billing** to see your month-to-date event count alongside your plan's monthly quota. The usage bar updates as events are ingested, so you can track how close you are to your limit throughout the month.

## Upgrading or downgrading your plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open billing settings">
    Navigate to **Settings > Billing** in your organization.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Upgrade (or Change plan)">
    Select the plan you want to move to. You'll be redirected to a Stripe-hosted checkout page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment in Stripe">
    Enter or confirm your payment details. Stripe handles all card processing — LumiqTrace never stores your card information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the plan change">
    Once Stripe confirms the payment, your new plan limits take effect immediately. Your dashboard will reflect the updated event quota, retention window, and member limits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  When upgrading mid-month, Stripe prorates the charge for the remaining days in your billing period. When downgrading, the lower limits take effect at the start of your next billing period.
</Tip>

## Managing payment and invoices

LumiqTrace uses the **Stripe Customer Portal** for all payment management. To access it:

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**.
2. Click **Manage billing** (or **Customer portal**).
3. You'll be redirected to Stripe, where you can:
   - Update your credit card or payment method
   - Download past invoices as PDFs
   - View upcoming invoice details
   - Update your billing address or tax ID

## Cancelling your subscription

<Warning>
  Cancellation is irreversible once the billing period ends. Downgrading to Free reduces your quota, retention, project, and member limits. Data older than 7 days will no longer be accessible after the retention window shrinks.
</Warning>

To cancel, open the Stripe Customer Portal from **Settings > Billing** and cancel your subscription there. Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period. After that, your organization moves to the Free plan:

- Event quota resets to 10K/month
- Data retention reduces to 7 days (older data becomes inaccessible)
- You are limited to 1 project and 1 member

If you have more than 1 project or more than 1 member at the time of downgrade, existing projects and members are not automatically removed — but you will need to reduce them to stay within Free plan limits to avoid restrictions.
